HOW TO PLAY
Survive the Grotto. The void hungers for you.

⟡ Objective

Defeat waves of enemies level by level. You play until you die — there is no final level. Enemies grow stronger and combine as levels progress. Achieve the highest level to etch your name in the Grotto Codex.

⟡ Each Turn

  • You start each turn with 3 Mana (purple orbs). Mana refreshes each turn.
  • Each card costs 1 Mana to play. You may play multiple cards per turn.
  • Click a card in your hand to SELECT it (it rises up). Click again or click the enemy to ATTACK.
  • When you play a card, your champion attacks the current active enemy.
  • Every card triggers its SKILL when played — read the tooltip carefully.
  • After playing or skipping, click END TURN to let enemies retaliate.

⟡ Skills Explained

  • Bait-and-Switch: Returns a card from discard to hand. Bonus mobility.
  • Shaft of Ruin: Deals 2 bonus damage + draws a card if kill.
  • Slumber-Shielding: Gain a Shield (absorb next hit) but lose 1 ATK this turn.
  • Axolotl's Toxin: Stun enemy for 1 turn (skip their attack) + 1 bonus damage.
  • Knowledge Scribe: Draw 2 extra cards from your deck this turn.
  • Shining Lure: All remaining enemies must attack you only — but you taunt them. High risk.
  • Corridor Vision: Reduce active enemy DEF by 2 + draw 1 card.
  • Cosmic Tapestry: Reshuffle your discard into the deck. +1 ATK bonus this turn.

⟡ Enemy Mechanics

  • Enemies have scaling skills too — they grow stronger on enemy turns.
  • At every 3rd level, enemies COMBINE — stats fuse and skills merge for a boss fight.
  • Defeat ALL enemies on the field to clear the level and advance.
  • After each level clear, you restore HP and draw fresh cards.

⟡ HP & Death

  • You have 3 Lives. Each time your HP hits 0, you lose one life and restore to 30 HP.
  • Lose all 3 lives and the run ends. Your level is recorded if you're logged in.
  • Enemy ATK deals damage to your HP directly unless you have a Shield.

⟡ Pro Tips

  • Wiki's Knowledge Scribe gives you card advantage — play it when your hand is low.
  • Grotto-Side Lure forces enemies to attack you — useful when enemies are nearly dead.
  • Potentate's Toxin stuns an enemy — combo with Corridor Vision to shred defense.
  • Enemy Glitch-Fire Lurker gains ATK every time YOU draw — manage your draws.
  • Cosmic Tapestry saves you from deck exhaustion — never let your deck go empty.
